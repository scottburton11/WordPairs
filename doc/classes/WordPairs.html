WordPairs

WordPairs outputs pairs of words with alternate spellings. This example produces word alternates by reversing a variable-length ending word segment.

ex: agonies, agonise, analog, angola, animal, lamina

WordPairs outputs comma-separated pairs to STDOUT.

Usage

  WordPairs.run(word_list, dictionary, trim_length)
